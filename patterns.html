HAVE YOU EVER SEEN ANY PATTERN ON RUBIK'S CUBE ? YES, YOU HEAR RIGHT RUBIK'S CUBES HAVE INTRESTING PATTERN





There are specific type of patterns. You can make them with very easy steps. You can also see NUMBERS with moves like - U AND U2this indicates that you need to do that specific move 2 times. Whatever number is written with your algorithm, you will have to move it that many times on the Rubik's Cube.
